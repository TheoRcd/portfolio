Here are the skills I cherish to design relevant, functional, inclusive, and delightful solutions.

Logical thinking

Most good designs are common sense, but it’s all about organising a lot of complex information to frame problems correctly for the right people to design simple solutions. I have experience turning ambiguity into clarity.

Looking for facts

I make efficient data-informed decisions built on facts collaborating with key experts, testing my designs with users, making data analysis, and collecting relevant insights to iterate towards the best solution.

Strong execution

Previous points are nothing without being able to materialise what a solution should look like. This is where I rely on my eye for details through visual design, micro-interactions, animations, accessiblity, and prototyping.

Design leadership

I’m comfortable designing a vision, setting team objectives and having ways to measure their impact, so we can ship solutions efficiently. I also have experience recruiting and mentoring designers.
